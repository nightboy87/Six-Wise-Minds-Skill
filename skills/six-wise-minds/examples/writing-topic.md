# Example: Writing Topic

## User input

我想写一篇公众号，主题是“AI 让我变成了更好用的人”，这个选题值得写吗？

## Expected behavior

- Trigger standard mode.
- White Hat asks what factual examples support it.
- Red Hat identifies reader resonance and discomfort.
- Black Hat warns against overgeneralization and unsupported claims.
- Yellow Hat identifies strong emotional hook.
- Green Hat suggests angles and titles.
- Blue Secretary checks whether the final advice is actionable.
- Chair recommends whether to write, how to narrow, and what evidence to add.
