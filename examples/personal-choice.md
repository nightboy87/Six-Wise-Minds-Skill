# Example: Personal Choice

## User input

我在纠结要不要离职。

## Expected behavior

- Trigger standard mode with safety and professional boundary.
- Chair should not decide for the user.
- Red Hat may identify emotional exhaustion, desire to be seen, or fear, but must not diagnose.
- White Hat lists missing facts: financial runway, alternatives, work conditions, health impact.
- Black Hat lists risks of impulsive resignation and risks of staying.
- Green Hat proposes lower-risk experiments.
- Chair recommends next action such as one-week evidence collection or conversation preparation.
