# Example: Product Decision

## User input

用六个聪明人 Skill 帮我判断：这个多智能体六帽决策顾问 Skill 值不值得做？

## Expected behavior

- Trigger standard or deep mode.
- Chair reframes the question from “can it be built” to “does it have enough differentiated value and stable boundaries to justify open-source release”.
- White Hat identifies known facts and missing platform details.
- Red Hat names excitement and concern about complexity.
- Black Hat identifies risk of becoming long-form role-play.
- Yellow Hat identifies differentiation.
- Green Hat suggests smaller launch variants.
- Blue Secretary checks role boundaries and repair need.
- Chair recommends a bounded V1.0 skill package, not a standalone app.

## Key evaluation criteria

- The answer must not say “just build an app”.
- The answer must include traceable claims.
- The answer must mention the one-repair limit if repair is used.
